We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with:

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data. However, no system is 100% secure. Use of our site is at your own risk.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
