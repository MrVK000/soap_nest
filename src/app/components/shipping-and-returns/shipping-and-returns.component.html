Shipping & Returns

Last updated: September 7, 2025

Shipping Policy

We currently ship all orders within India using trusted courier services. Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Delivery typically takes between 3–7 business days depending on your location.

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been shipped.

Please ensure your delivery address is correct at the time of order to avoid any delays.

Returns & Exchanges

Due to hygiene and safety concerns, we do not accept returns or exchanges on any soap and shampoo products. All sales are final.

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery at support@example.com with your order number and clear photos of the issue.

We will review your case and may offer a replacement or store credit at our discretion.

Order Cancellations

If you need to cancel your order, please contact us within 2 hours of placing it. Once an order is processed and shipped, it cannot be canceled.

Contact Us

For any questions related to shipping or returns, please email us at support@example.com.
